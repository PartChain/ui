PartChain is a blockchain-based system for tracking parts along the supply chain.

A high level of transparency across the supplier network enables faster intervention based on recorded event in the supply chain.

This saves costs by seamlessly tracking parts and creates trust through clearly defined and secure data access by the companies and persons involved in the process.

PartChain UI

This app here is PartChain UI. It allows you to find parts in the supply chain and access the data stored in the blockchain for a particular part.

In case of quality problems, parts can be locked and released again after a problem has been solved.

The app can be used on the PC as well as on all tablet devices with a browser. It works best with Google Chrome and iOS Safari.
